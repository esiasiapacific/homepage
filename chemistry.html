Technology Overview

The Iron Flow battery consists of iron, salt and water chemistry and can be either deployed in containers or in a large-scale grid-connected warehouse.

Iron Chloride Flow Battery

  • Also known as Redox Flow Battery (reduction-oxidation)
  • Electrochemical storage device with unlimited charge/discharge cycles
  • Electrical energy drives chemical change in the electrolyte, which flows through electrochemical cell
