Our renewable
energy future

— today

We provide reliable and environmentally friendly renewable energy storage solutions that are essential for Australia's transition to a renewable energy future.

Energy Storage Industries - Asia Pacific (ESI) is fully integrated — we manufacture, install, maintain and finance energy storage solutions.

By 2026, ESI will deliver 400 megawatts (MW) of energy storage each year for the next 15 years — that is six gigawatts (GW) of energy storage, or 20 per cent of Australia's renewable energy needs.
