Technology Overveiw

The DESS technology chosen is from ESS,Inc (NYSE:GWH) a Portland, US based company, producing an Iron- Flow battery. The device consists of iron, salt and water chemistry and can _be either deployed in containers or in a large scale grid connected warehouse.

Iron Chloride Flow Battery

  • Also known as Redox Flow Battery (reduction-oxidation)
  • Electrochemical storage device with unlimited charge/discharge cycles
  • Electrical energy drives chemical change in the electrolyte, which flows through electrochemical cell

During charging:

\(\ce{Fe^{II}Cl2 + 2e- <-> Fe + 2Cl-}\) -0.44V (negative electrode)

\(\ce{2Fe^{II}Cl2 + Cl- <-> 2Fe^{III}Cl3 + 2e-}\) +0.77V (positive electrode)

During discharge: equations reverse
