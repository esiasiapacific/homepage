At Energy Storage Industries — Asia Pacific (ESI), we provide reliable and environmentally friendly renewable energy storage solutions that are essential for Australia's transition to a renewable energy future.

ESI is fully integrated — we manufacture, install, maintain and finance energy storage solutions.

We are investing up to $70 million to manufacture and distribute low-cost, long-life iron flow batteries that allow large-scale energy storage [link to the our products and services page] for wholesale electricity generators, energy retailers, and commercial and industrial (C&I) customers.

We also investing in the manufacture of small, modular distributed energy storage units that can be installed in low voltage networks and microgrids, and behind the meter installations for C&I customers.

By 2026, ESI will deliver 400 megawatts (MW) of energy storage each year for the next 15 years — that is six gigawatts (GW) of energy storage, or 20 per cent of Australia's renewable energy needs.

We are a Queensland-based, 100 per cent Australian-owned company. We have a research and development facility at the National Battery Testing Centre [when created, this will link to the NBTC page] in Brisbane.

Construction has started on our manufacturing facility at Maryborough, [when created, this will link to the Maryborough page] and we will soon expand our operations with a second facility at Townsville [when created, this will link to Townsville page]. ESI plans to export renewable energy storage solutions to Asia Pacific within the next six years.
