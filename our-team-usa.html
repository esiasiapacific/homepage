USA TEAM

Craig E. Evans - President, CEO, & Founder

Mr. Craig Evans is the President, CEO, and a founder of ESS Inc. Having started the company from his garage in 2011, he led the company to the commercialization of the iron flow battery system in 2016. Prior to ESS Inc., Mr. Evans was the Director of Design & Product Development at ClearEdge Power Inc. His team designed the ClearEdge5, a 5kW combine-heat-and-power fuel cell system, which was successfully commercialized in 2010. This product received both CSA International certification in North America and KNREC certification in Asia.

Mr. Evans began his career at United Technologies Corporation (UTC) where he held technical leadership positions in their Fuel Cell business and, prior to that, as a project engineer in the Pratt & Whitney aircraft engine business. In 2006, Mr. Evans was awarded the distinguished Georg Mead Award, the highest honor for individual engineering achievement at UTC. Mr. Evans has authored over 15 patents and patent applications. He holds a BS in Aerospace Engineering and a MS in Mechanical Engineering from Clarkson University, a MS in Finance from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute and a M.B.A from Carnegie Mellon University.

Dr. Julia Song - Chief Technology Officer & Founder

Dr. Song, a co-founder of ESS Inc., formally joined ESS in April of 2012 as the Chief Technology Officer. In this position, she has spearheaded the transition of the iron flow battery technology to becoming a commercial energy storage system. Prior to joining ESS Inc., Dr. Song was the Vice President of Research and Development at ClearEdge Power Inc., where she led over 20 cross-functional scientists and engineers on all corporate R&D projects and the commercialization of the 5 kW Combined Heat and Power Fuel Cell system.

Dr. Song started her career at Milliken & Co. as an Industrial Research Chemist. Dr. Song has authored approximately 20 peer-reviewed publications and over 10 patents and patent applications. Dr. Song holds a B.S. in Chemistry from Peking University and a Ph.D. in Chemistry from University of North Carolina-Chapel Hill. She specializes in fuel cells, batteries, electrochemistry and the catalysis properties of nanoparticles.

Amir Moftakhar - Chief Financial Officer

Mr. Moftakhar is the Chief Financial Officer for ESS Inc. Mr. Moftakhar is an accomplished finance executive with experience in energy, banking, technology, real estate, and consumer products specializing in strategy and finance. Mr. Moftakhar has significant corporate experience in finance, business development, strategy, and M&A with companies including Bank of America, Sempra Energy, San Diego Gas and Electric, and Cox Communications.

He has been a lecturer and adjunct professor in the areas of economics, finance, statistics, and business, including his latest experience as an Adjunct Professor of Finance with the University of San Diego. Mr. Moftakhar received his Bachelor's of Managerial Economics from the University of California, Davis, his Master's in Business Administration from the Graziadio School of Business at Pepperdine University, and his Executive Master of Leadership degree from the Price School of Public Policy at the University of Southern California.

Hugh McDermott - Senior Vice President Business Development & Sales

Mr. McDermott is the Senior Vice President Business Development & Sales for ESS Inc. An energy sector executive focused on growing energy infrastructure and advanced energy technology businesses primarily addressing utility, industrial and commercial sectors, Mr. McDermott has served in numerous executive and leadership roles throughout his career in high-growth start-up companies as well as executive roles in public listed companies.

Prior to joining ESS, Inc., Mr. McDermott was the Vice President Sales for Silver Spring Networks where he successfully led the company's market-building presence and key partnership development for Smart Grid and Smart City business across the APAC region. As Global Head of Business Development at Better Place, Mr. McDermott created the world's first smart charging commercial offering which leveraged networked electric vehicle charging infrastructure to provide dynamic load management and energy storage services to utilities, grid operators and energy traders. Mr. McDermott is a Hokie from VA Tech where he earned his BS in Mechanical Engineering in Power Systems.

Matt Berkebile - Vice President of Manufacturing & Operations

Mr. Berkebile is the Vice President of Manufacturing & Operations for ESS Inc. Mr. Berkebile brings deep technical and operational background in engineering, R&D, product design, and manufacture of advanced technologies in the power generation and aviation sectors. Mr. Berkebile specializes in startup and acquisition restructuring, optimization, and operational growth and his experience includes serving in global and executive leadership roles in both public and privately-held companies including General Electric and Mitsubishi Hitachi Power Systems.

Mr. Berkebile has authored over 10 patents and patent applications and holds a BS in Mechanical Engineering from University of South Florida. He is a veteran of the US Navy where he served 5 years as an Aircrew Search and Rescue Swimmer.

Brian Lisiecki - Vice President of Information & Business Systems

Mr. Lisiecki is currently the Vice President of Information & Business systems for ESS Inc. He has led the initiative to standardize cross functional business processes, including enabling Oracle ERP to provide scalability and better decision insights.

Prior to joining ESS Inc., Mr. Lisiecki spent 19 years at the Campbell Soup Company in various technology and finance leadership roles. These roles included Business Intelligence, SAP, Business Acquisition Integrations, Global FP&A and Financial Consolidation. Mr. Lisiecki holds a B.S. in Management Information Systems from Clarkson University and a M.S. in Management Information Systems from Pennsylvania State University.
