Maryborough

Work on our $70 million Maryborough facility officially started on Wednesday, 6 July 2022.

Deputy Premier the Hon Dr Steven Miles MP, State Member for Maryborough Bruce Saunders MP, Fraser Coast Regional Council Mayor Cr George Seymour and Butchulla elder Eleanor Currie were on hand to officiate at a sod turning ceremony to mark the start of construction.

Once complete in 2024, the Maryborough facility will manufacture and distribute iron flow batteries that allow large-scale energy storage for wholesale electricity generators, energy retailers, and commercial and industrial customers.
